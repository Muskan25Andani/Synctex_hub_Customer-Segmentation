Customer Segmentation – Cluster Report
Overview

This project segments mall customers using K-Means clustering based on age, annual income, 
and spending behavior. The Elbow Method identified 5 optimal clusters, representing distinct 
customer groups. These segments help businesses design targeted marketing strategies.

Cluster 0 – High Income, High Spending Customers

Profile:
High annual income, High spending score, Typically premium customers

Business Insight:
These customers contribute the most revenue and are brand loyal.

Marketing Action:
Offer premium memberships, Exclusive discounts, Early access to new products

Cluster 1 – Low Income, Low Spending Customers

Profile:
Low income
Low spending score
Price-sensitive customers

Business Insight:
This group is cautious with spending and responds well to affordability.

Marketing Action:
Provide discounts and coupons, Promote budget-friendly products, Seasonal offers

Cluster 2 – High Income, Low Spending Customers

Profile:
High income
Low spending score
Careful or selective buyers

Business Insight:
These customers have purchasing power but need persuasion.

Marketing Action:
Personalized recommendations, Premium product awareness campaigns, Loyalty programs

Cluster 3 – Low Income, High Spending Customers

Profile:
Low income
High spending score
Impulsive or trend-driven buyers

Business Insight:
Despite limited income, they spend actively.

Marketing Action:
Limited-time offers, Flash sales, Trend-based promotions

Cluster 4 – Average Income, Average Spending Customers

Profile:
Moderate income
Moderate spending score
Regular customers

Business Insight:
This is a stable customer base with consistent purchases.

Marketing Action:
Retention strategies, Bundled product offers, Reward points programs

Conclusion
Customer segmentation enables personalized marketing and better customer engagement. 
By identifying different spending behaviors, businesses can optimize promotions, 
improve customer satisfaction, and increase overall revenue.
